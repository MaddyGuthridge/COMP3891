Threads are:
* Unit of execution within [[process]]es
* Used within programs to keep working during blocking operations (disk, network)

## Properties

### Process management
- Registers
- [[program counter]]
- Stack pointer
- [[process state]]

### Memory management
- Pointer to stack segment
